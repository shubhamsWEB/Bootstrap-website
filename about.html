OUR VIGOR LIES IN OUR PASSION TO

EDUCATE


Innolat Technologies Private Limited is an innovative education technology startup which is passionately committed to solve the problem of employability in graduates from tier-2/3 cities of India. Our cloud-based technology platform – iLrnn acts as a Personalized Virtual Classroom by facilitating self-paced learning blended with collaborative virtual interaction with industry-based mentors and peers. Students learn industry relevant skills while working on live internship projects during a year-long engagement. “Over 80% of engineering graduates in India are unemployable” – Study

There is a huge gap between industry expectations and the current skill level of graduates in India. Innolat believes that the key lies in connecting the dots between offline and online learning by combining the unique merits of both to offer a personalized learning experience.

Innolat conducts Fundamental Skills in IT program in association with SSC NASSCOM for employability enhancement of engineering undergraduates to make them industry-ready.

IT-ITeS Sector Skills Council NASSCOM (SSC NASSCOM) the skill standard setting body of the IT-BPM Industry which is an integral part of NASSCOM is the education & skill development initiative of NASSCOM; works with its industry members and select academic and skill development institutions to help improve the quality and quantity of the employable workforce available to this industry.

Our flagship Holistic Employability Assessment Test (HEAT)evaluatesstudents’ skill level on critical areas like aptitude skills, communication skills, logical reasoning, domain-specific technical skills as well as knowledge about latest technologies thus measuring the existing gaps of every student.
